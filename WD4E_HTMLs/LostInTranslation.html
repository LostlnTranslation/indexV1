About Me

Hello world, my name is Christian. Born and raised in Switzerland, I have always been a restless soul.
I was very lucky to be able to travel extensively in my 20s and early 30s. This allowed me to discover many parts of this beautiful planet.
But I always dreamed of one day settling down somewhere permanently.
Last year, I finally decided to stop letting fear and uncertainty hold me back. So I sold all my things. Packed the few belongings I had left in my backpack and finally hit the road.

Now I would like to invite you all to join me on what is probably my most adventurous journey yet as I start a new life in Bangkok, Thailand.
